This solution routes high-discount Opportunities through a two-stage approval —
sales manager, then finance — locking the record during review and notifying the
submitter at each step.

## What it demonstrates

- Declarative approval design with entry criteria
- Queue-based routing to a finance team
- Record locking for data integrity

## How to validate

Create an Opportunity with a discount over 20% and submit it for approval. Watch
it route through both stages. The full build is documented in the linked write-up.
